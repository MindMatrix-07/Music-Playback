Privacy Policy and Terms

Last Updated: 2026-01-26

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Music Playback ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers.

2. Data Collection & Storage

2.1. Local Storage (On Your Device)

We use your browser's Local Storage to verify settings and preferences. This data never leaves your device.

  • Spotify Authentication: Tokens (access_token, refresh_token) are stored locally to keep you logged in.
  • YouTube API Key: If you provide a custom API Key, it is stored locally (yt_api_key) and used directly by your browser to communicate with YouTube.

2.2. Third-Party Services

This App acts as a client to various third-party services. When you use the App, you interact directly with these platforms:

  • Spotify: Authentication and metadata. Spotify Privacy Policy
  • Discord: User Authentication. Discord Privacy Policy
  • Apple Music: Search and playback. Apple Privacy Policy
  • YouTube: Video playback. Google Privacy Policy
  • Wikidata: Artist images.
  • LRCLIB: Lyrics retrieval.

3. How We Use Information

  • Search Queries: Sent directly to Spotify or Apple Music APIs to fetch results.
  • Playback: Handled via secure IFrame embeds provided by the respective platforms.
  • Lyrics: Song title and artist name are sent to LRCLIB to retrieve lyrics.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We use Vercel Analytics to monitor website performance and speed. This data is aggregated and anonymous. We do not track individual user behavior or share personal data.

Third-party embeds (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube) may use their own cookies to maintain user sessions and preferences, subject to their respective policies.

5. Access Control & Account Binding

This is a private-access application. Access is controlled via a unique "Access Code" provided by the administrator. This code is linked to you in one of two ways:

A. Discord Binding (Recommended)

  • We use Discord to verify your identity.
  • When you log in, we store your unique Discord User ID in our private Access Sheet.
  • Benefit: This grants you "Lifetime Access" across any device. You can log in on your phone or computer, and your code will work everywhere.
  • Data Collected: We only access your Discord ID, Username, and Avatar. We do not access your servers, friends, or messages.

B. Device Binding (Local Auth)

  • If you choose "Continue without Discord", we generate a random Device ID for your browser.
  • Limitation: Your access is tied strictly to that specific browser.
  • Risk: If you clear your browser cookies, reinstall the app, or switch devices, your access and any saved API Keys will be lost.
  • We do not collect any personal data for this method.

C. Strict One-Time Use Policy

  • Security Rule: An Access Code is strictly bound to the first User ID (Discord) or Device ID that claims it.
  • No Sharing: Once used, a code cannot be used by any other user or device.
  • Re-Entry: The original owner (same Discord account) can reuse their own code to log in on new devices.

D. Administrator Controls

  • Blocking: Administrators reserve the right to block access codes or users at any time for abuse or policy violations. Blocking is effective immediately.
  • Maintenance Mode: Administrators may temporarily disable access to the application for upgrades or safety reasons. During this time, the app will be inaccessible.
  • Data Deletion: Administrators have the capability to permanently delete user data (access codes and bindings) upon request or for account management. This action is irreversible.

Diagnostic Tools

  • The App includes a manual "Copy Diagnostic Info" feature. This data is manual-only and never sent automatically.
  • Security: Sensitive credentials (like YouTube API Keys) are automatically scrubbed and completely excluded from these logs before generation.

6. Security

  • Minimal Storage: We use a secure MongoDB Database for primary access control, with Google Sheets used only as a secondary backup.
  • Client-Side Requests: API requests are primarily made from your browser to the service provider.
  • Data Hardening: We employ strict Anti-Theft and Anti-Caching protocols.
    • All API responses are sent with Cache-Control: no-store headers, ensuring your data is not saved by browsers or intermediate proxies.
    • Requests are validated to ensure they originate only from our authentic website.
    • Content Protection: We implement technical measures to prevent the unauthorized downloading of copyrighted assets (images, audio) to comply with third-party licensing agreements.

7. Your Rights

You can clear your data at any time by:

  1. Deleting your Account Data: Use the "Delete Account" button in Settings to permanently remove your Discord binding from our database.
  2. Administrator Request: You may contact the administrator to request manual deletion of your data. This action is identical to the self-deletion option and removes your access code permanently.
  3. Logging out (clears Spotify tokens).
  4. Deleting your YouTube API Key in settings.
  5. Clearing your browser's cache/local storage.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our features or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here.
